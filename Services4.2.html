General Services

Outpatient Consultations

Our outpatient department offers consultations with specialists across various fields, ensuring personalized care and treatment plans without requiring hospitalization.

General Surgery

We provide a range of general surgical services, from minor outpatient procedures to major inpatient surgeries, performed by our highly skilled surgeons.

Diagnostic Imaging (MRI, CT Scan, X-Ray)

Our diagnostic imaging services include MRI, CT scans, and X-rays, using state-of-the-art equipment to help diagnose a wide range of medical conditions with precision.

Laboratory Services (Blood Tests, Biopsies)

We offer comprehensive laboratory services, including blood tests, biopsies, and other diagnostic procedures, providing accurate results to aid in patient diagnosis and treatment.

Dental Care and Oral Surgery

Our dental clinic provides routine dental care as well as specialized oral surgery, including treatments for complex dental issues and cosmetic procedures.

Eye Care and Vision Correction (LASIK, Cataract Surgery)

We offer advanced eye care services, including vision correction procedures such as LASIK and cataract surgery, ensuring optimal eye health and clear vision.

Orthopedic Services (Fractures, Joint Replacement)

Our orthopedic department treats a variety of musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, joint replacements, and sports injuries, using both surgical and non-surgical methods.

Cardiology (Heart Checkups, ECG)

Our cardiology team offers regular heart checkups, ECGs, and advanced treatments for heart diseases, with a focus on preventive care and effective disease management.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

Our physical therapy and rehabilitation services help patients recover from injuries or surgeries, improving mobility and quality of life through tailored rehabilitation programs.

Pediatric Care

We provide specialized pediatric care, offering consultations, vaccinations, and treatments for a variety of health conditions in children, ensuring their growth and well-being.

Vaccinations and Immunizations

We offer a full range of vaccinations and immunizations for both children and adults, helping to protect against a variety of diseases and maintain community health.

Endoscopy and Colonoscopy

Our gastroenterology department offers endoscopy and colonoscopy services to diagnose and treat various digestive conditions, ensuring patient comfort and safety.

Gynecology and Women's Health Services

Our women's health services include gynecological exams, prenatal care, and treatments for reproductive health conditions, ensuring comprehensive care for women of all ages.

Psychiatry and Counseling Services

Our mental health department provides psychiatric consultations, therapy, and counseling services to help individuals manage mental health conditions and improve their well-being.

Nutrition and Dietary Counseling

Our nutrition and dietary experts provide personalized counseling, helping patients develop healthy eating habits and manage conditions like diabetes, obesity, and heart disease.
