24-Hour Services

Book Appointment

Emergency Room (ER) and Trauma Care

Our ER and trauma care units are available around the clock to handle any medical emergencies. Our team of professionals is equipped to provide immediate treatment in life-threatening situations.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

The ICU offers 24/7 monitoring and treatment for critically ill patients. Our ICU is staffed by a dedicated team of experts using advanced technology to provide the highest level of care.

24-Hour Ambulance Service

We provide a 24-hour ambulance service for emergency patient transport. Our ambulances are equipped with life-saving medical equipment and staffed by trained paramedics.

Pharmacy Services

Our on-site pharmacy operates 24 hours a day, ensuring that patients receive their prescribed medications promptly, whether for urgent treatments or ongoing care.

Labor and Delivery Services

We offer 24/7 labor and delivery services for expectant mothers. Our team of obstetricians, midwives, and nurses ensures safe deliveries and immediate postnatal care.

Critical Care and Cardiac Monitoring

Our critical care unit provides round-the-clock monitoring and treatment for patients with severe health conditions, including continuous cardiac monitoring for those with heart-related issues.
